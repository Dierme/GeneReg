    About

    This project aims to provide utility of working with popular services for finding gene regulating miRNAs such as TargetScan, miRanda mirWalk and ability to complex analyze its data

    Example

    Maybe you want to find all miRNA which can inhibit *gene1*, *gene2*...*geneN* or you have a concrete pathway. Sure! This application can provide you with an answer using popular services for finding miRNA targets. Results will be provided in tables and graphics with numbers or miRNA and targeted sites for each gene.
